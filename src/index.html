      Welcome!

      In the modern world it’s a really important skill to be able to differentiate ‘signal’ from ‘noise’. The majority of the things that we do on a daily basis, do not move the needle forward, they do not make our boat go faster. The minimalistic approach to product management is about limiting the time and resources you spent on ‘maintenance tasks’ and maximizing the time and resources you spent on adding value.
